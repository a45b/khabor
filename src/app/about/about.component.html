About

khabor (খবৰ) means NEWS in ASSAMESE.

khabor is a NEWS app kind of like google news.

it's build with Angular and Powered by newsapi.org

Advantage of khabor are:

  • No personal data of the user is required. That's why I didn't added weather info as it required the visitor IP or Geo Location
  • Newsapi will gives the same result for all. Doesn't matter who, where, what you are!

Selected INDIA as default country as I'm INDIAN. You can change it from settings

Disclaimer: I am not promoting newsapi.org in any way. I am using there Developer Option which is free for all non-commercial projects.
